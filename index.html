Ongoing

We made Ongoing tasks specifically to help reduce your Overdue tasks. We like to think of Ongoing tasks as ones that important enough to not forget about or to delete, but also not important to just bog down your day-to-day. If you get around to it today, great. If not, no big deal.

Some exmaples of these are:

  • Find a new support staff
  • Try a new email provider
  • Test out new food delivery app
